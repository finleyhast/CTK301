Short acoustic thing using Drake's Marvins Room. I spent a lot of time debating adding more to this because it feels kind of empty or like I didn't do enough work, but I didn't want to overcrowd it. This is the first musical composition I've made in college that I don't think is utterly terrible and that's enough for me.

For this, I was asked to create a narrative using only sound effects, no dialogue. I wanted to tell a story of a man unexpectedly pulled out of his life into a fantasy realm and feel I was quite successful.

For my final CTK 203 project, I wanted to do another foley/sound effects narrative. I really enjoy stories that begin normally and reach an absurdist conclusion, so I wanted to try to capture that with my project. We follow a character going about their day, getting up and ready and leaving the house. They arrive at their destination and discover a fight happening, so they intervene. There is then a "mission complete" sound cue to allude to a video game-type story and remove it from our reality, despite the normal beginning.
